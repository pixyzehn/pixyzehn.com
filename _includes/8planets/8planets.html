8Planets

8Planets is a simple viewer for planetary orbits in the Solar System.

It's now available in public beta. With TestFlight is installed, open this link.

  • Speed up or slow down time to see how the planets rotate over time.
  • Travel back or forward in time to view the Solar System on any given date.
  • Widgets allow you to display the real-time Solar System on your Home screen.
  • Share your favorite moment of the Solar System.
